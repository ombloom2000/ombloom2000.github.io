BROCK COLLECTION

Today’s collection had the luxe fabrics we’ve come to expect—it’s a treat to get an up-close look at whatever floral silks and brocades the Brocks have dug up at their French mill—but in noticeably simpler, less decorative shapes. Backstage before the show, Laura said she’d wanted the clothes to exude a stronger, more straightforward confidence. You can certainly feel powerful in a barely there slip or ruffly corset, but it’s not a look most women can wear every day. Fall’s soft tailoring and mix-and-match separates felt just as special, but will be easier to wear. Several looks that would have been styled with stilettos or pointy mules in the past were grounded by flat suede or low-heeled leather boots—even a ruched silk LBD. The combo looked a little imperfect, another hint that the Brocks are tuning into “real women’s” evolving tastes. -Emily Farra, Vogue.com
